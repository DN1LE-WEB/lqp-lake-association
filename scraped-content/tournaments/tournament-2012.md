## **33 rd Annual Lac Qui Parle Walleye Tournament**    **2012 Results**            We would like to thank everyone who participated in the 33rd Annual Walleye Tournament, and all who came out to watch.  We'd especially like to thank our sponsors, because without them, this wouldn't be possible.  We had great weather on Saturday, and Sunday wasn't too bad except for a few sprinkles at weigh in time!  Okay, maybe more than a few sprinkles!  This year we had 51 teams that caught 162 fish on day one weighing in a total of 365 pounds.  Day two, was a little tougher, 127 fish were caught weighing in a total of 258 pounds.  So, a two day total of 623 pounds.
