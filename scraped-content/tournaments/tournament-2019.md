40th Annual LQP Lake Walleye Tournament

​

Lac Qui Parle Lake

Saturday, June 8th, 2019

​

**The 40th Annual Lac qui Parle Lake Walleye Tournament was greeted with strong South winds and warm temperatures.  60 boats headed out in hopes of bringing 5 fish to the scales.  45 walleyes were weighed in for a total of 116.9 lbs.  Tony Malmquist and Jordan Fischer took top honors weighing in 4 fish for 9.64 lbs and took home a check for $2,200.  Second Place went to Mike Halvorson and Josh Fernholz who weighed 1 fish for 7.7 lbs.  This was also the big fish of the tournament so they took home $1700 in prize money.  Adam Spray and Eric Spray took third place weighing in 3 fish for 7.6 pounds good for $900 plus $510 in option pot money.  Fourth place was Ryan Hoium and Lucas Hoium who weighed 2 fish for 7.32 lbs worth $600 plus $306 in option pot money.  Fifth place went to Matt Fernholz and Grady Fernholz who weighed 1 fish for 6.84 lbs, they also had the second largest walleye and option pot winnings for a total of $904.**

**Thank you to all those who competed, we hope to see you again next year.  We would also like to Thank our tournament director Susan Stensrud, her husband Wayne and Andy Johnson for all the work they did during the tournament.**
