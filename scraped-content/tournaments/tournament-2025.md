45th Annual LQP Lake Walleye Tournament

​

## **The 45th Annual Lac qui Parle Lake Association Walleye Tournament took place on Saturday, June 7th.  48 boats headed out in hopes of bringing 5 fish to the scales.  146 walleyes were weighed in for a total of 377.29 lbs.  Jamie Jahn and Mitch Lillejord took top honors weighing in 5 fish for 17.27 lbs, including  the second biggest walleye of the day weighing 8.85 lbs and took home a check for $3,900, which included option pot money and early bird incentive money.  Second Place went to Pete McGinty and Cody Enger who weighed 4 fish for 16.85 lbs, they took home $1910.00 in prize money, which included option pot money.  Corey Joyce and Steve Benson took third place weighing in 4 fish for 15.2 pounds good for $1,410, which included option pot money.  Fourth place was Bob Hoium and Rick Hoium who weighed 5 fish for 14.96 lbs worth $615..  Fifth place went to Gordy Brehmer and Chuck Ronglien who weighed 5 fish for 14.57 lbs, good for $492 in prize money.**    **Thank you to all those who competed, we hope to see you again next year.  We would also like to Thank our tournament director Brett Jacobs.  We would also like to Thank board member Andy Johnson and his family, along with Marcia Golden and Scott Monson for all of their help at weigh-ins and in preparing for this year's tournament.**
