41st Annual LQP Lake Walleye Tournament

Cancelled due to COVID-19

​

May 7, 2020

The Lac qui Parle Lake Association Board of Directors has made the decision to CANCEL this year’s tournament. This was a difficult decision, due to the uncertainty of the COVID-19 virus and our Governor’s orders. We made this decision with the safety or our volunteers and fishermen in mind.

Thank you ALL for your understanding and support. We look forward to seeing everyone at next year’s tournament.

Checks will be mailed back as soon as we can.

Looking forward to next year’s tournament, we will use the current 2020 roster, plus the 5 teams on the waiting list who have paid, as the teams with first chance to get into the 2021 tournament.
