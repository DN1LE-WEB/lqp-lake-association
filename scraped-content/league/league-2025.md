Week #1 Results

The first week of fishing league saw 25 walleyes brought to the scale for 52.1 lbs.  Taking top honors was Corey Joyce and Jon Jerve(sub) weighing in 5 fish for  16.91 lbs.  They also had the big fish of the night,  a 8.02 pounder.  Second place went to Eric Spray and Adam Spray with 5 fish for 8.6 lbs.  Third place was Josh Weckwerth and Trent Weckwerth with 2 fish for 6.15 lbs.  Fourth place was Tom Ludvigson and Dustin Fernholz with 1 fish for 4.89 lbs.  Fifth place was Sedric Borsgard and Ryan Anderson with 2 fish for 3.37 lbs.

​

Week #2 Results

After a week off due to weather, the second week of fishing league saw 40 walleyes brought to the scale for 101.76 lbs.  Taking top honors was Eric Spray and Adam Spray weighing in 4 fish for  17.27 lbs.  Second place went to Logan Spray and Mikkal Erickson with 5 fish for 15.22 lbs.  Third place was Dave Cornelius and Steffan Sticklestad with 4 fish for 10.01 lbs.  Fourth place was Cody Enger and Patrick Miller with 5 fish for 9.70 lbs.  Fifth place was Corey Reiffenberger and Lainey Reiffenberger with 1 fish for 9.20 lbs.  Lainey had the big fish of the night!

​

​ Week #3 Results

The third week of fishing league saw 13 walleyes brought to the scale for 29.3 lbs.  Taking top honors was Josh Weckwerth and Trent Weckwerth weighing in 4 fish for  7.28 lbs.  Second place went to Logan Spray and Mikkal Erickson with 2 fish for 5.96 lbs.  They also had the big fish of the night a 4.41 lber.  Third place was Corey Joyce and Steve Benson with 2 fish for 5.23 lbs.  Fourth place was Garret Suchanek and Zach Suchanek with 1 fish for 3.23 lbs.  Fifth place was Eric Spray and Adam Spray with 1 fish for 2.55 lbs.

​ Week #4 Results

The Fourth week of fishing league saw 45 walleyes brought to the scale for 91.8 lbs.  Taking top honors was Eric Spray and Adam Spray weighing in 5 fish for  12.10 lbs.  Second place went to Steve Dahle and Jamie Jahn with 5 fish for 11.91 lbs.  Third place was Garret Suchanek and Zach Suchanek with 4 fish for 9.78 lbs.  Fourth place was Pete McGinty and Shannon Larson with 5 fish for 9.55 lbs.  Fifth place was Cody Enger and Patrick Miller with 5 fish for 7.89 lbs.  Big fish of the night went to Sedric Borsgard and Ryan Anderson a 5.12 lber.

Week #5 Results

The fifth week of fishing league saw 49 walleyes brought to the scale for 118.47 lbs.  Taking top honors was Logan Spray and Mikkal Erickson weighing in 5 fish for  17.98 lbs.  They also had the big fish of the night an 8.87 lber.  Second place went to Steve Dahle and Jamie Jahn with 5 fish for 15.88 lbs.  Third place was Tom Halvorson and Gage Halvorson with 4 fish for 14.00 lbs.  Fourth place was Corey Heinrich and Jerry Heinrich with 4 fish for 11.79 lbs.  Fifth place was Corey Reiffenberger and Lainey Reiffenberger with 3 fish for 9.56 lbs.  Next week will be the final week of fishing league.

Week #6 Results

The sixth week of fishing league saw 21 walleyes brought to the scale for 48.82 lbs.  Taking top honors was Jack Anderson and Tony Meseck weighing in 1 fish for  10.56 lbs.  They also had the big fish of the night.  Second place went to Austin Hiepler and Bob Hoium with 1 fish for 5.52 lbs.  Third place was Logan Spray and Mikkal Erickson with 3 fish for 5.32 lbs.  Fourth place was Tom Halvorson and Gage Halvorson with 2 fish for 4.76 lbs.  Fifth place was Garrett Suchanek and Zach Suchanek with 2 fish for 4.54 lbs.

End of the Year Results

The 2025 fishing league has come to an end.  This year's league was cut short by one week due to weather.  There were  193 walleyes weighed in for a total of  442.25 pounds.  Big Fish of the year was 10.56 lbs caught by Jack Anderson and Tony Meseck.  This year's first place team was Eric Spray and Adam Spray with a total weight of 51.42 lbs.  Second Place went to Logan Spray and Mikkal Erickson who had a total weight of 47.93 lbs.  Third Place was Corey Joyce and Steve Benson with a total weight of 35.49 lbs.  Congratulations to the top 3 teams.  Thank you to Corey Joyce, Corey Reiffenberger and Steve Benson for running the fishing league, it wouldn't happen without you guys!
