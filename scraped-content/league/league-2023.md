Week #1 Results

The first week of fishing saw 46 walleyes brought to the scale for 122.3125 lbs.  Taking top honors was Sam Thomas and Scott VanOverbeke weighing in 5 fish for 16.375 lbs.  Second place went to Tom Ludvigson and Dustin Fernholz with 5 fish for 16.1875 lbs. They also had the big fish of the night, a 6.875 pounder. Third place was Trent Jorgenson and Matt Norby with 5 fish for 12.4375 lbs.  Fourth place was Josh Nordaune and Steve Nordaune with 4 fish for 9.5 lbs.  Fifth place was Jeff Baker and Nick Baker with 3 fish for 8.375 lbs.  Sixth Place went to Dylan Hiepler and Sam Kaderlik with 3 fish for 7.4375 lbs.

Week #2 Results

The second week of fishing saw 34 walleyes brought to the scale for 112.625 lbs.  First place was Jamie Jahn and sub Jordan Jahn weighing in 3 fish for 17.1875 lbs.  They also had the big fish of the night, a 10.25 lb walleye.  Second place went to Gage Halvorson and sub Larry Halvorson with 4 fish for 15.25 lbs.  Third place was Dylan Hiepler and Sam Kaderlik with 1 fish for 8.75 lbs.  Fourth place was Corey Reiffenberger and Lainey Reiffenberger with 2 fish for 7.625 lbs.   Fifth place went to Tony Malmquist and Jordan Fischer with 1 fish for 7.5 lbs.  Sixth place was Austin Hiepler and sub Chad Hiepler weighing 3 fish for 7.25 lbs.

​ Week #3 Results

The third week of fishing proved to be a tough bite for most teams with only 17 walleyes brought to the scale for 40.375 lbs.  First place was Dylan Hiepler and Sam Kaderlik weighing in 3 fish for 10.625 lbs.  They also had the big fish of the night, a 6.5 lb walleye.  Second place went to Josh Weckwerth and Trent Weckwerth with 3 fish for 6.0625 lbs.  Third place was Trent Jorgenson and Matt Norby with 2 fish for 4.1875 lbs.  Fourth place was Tom Ludvigson and Dustin Fernholz with 1 fish for 3.75 lbs.   Fifth place went to Matt Fischer and Jeff Reike with 1 fish for 3.5 lbs.  Sixth place was Jon Jerve and Tony Omacht weighing 1 fish for 2.5625 lbs.

Week #6 Results

Week #7 Results

**​**

End of the Year Results

The 2023 fishing league has come to an end.  There were  173 walleyes weighed in for a total of  437.125 pounds.  This year's first place team was Dylan Hiepler and Sam Kaderlick with a total weight of 38.5 lbs.  Second Place went to  Tom Halvorson and Gage Halvorson  who had a total weight of 35.125 lbs.  Third Place was Tom Ludvigson and Dustin Fernholz with a total weight of 33.875 lbs.  Congratulations to the top 3 teams.  Thank you to Jeff Randall for running the fishing league!
