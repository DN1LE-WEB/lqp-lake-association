End of the Year Results

This year's fishing league was cut short to 5 weeks due to inclement weather.  There were 139 walleyes weighed for 336.75 lbs, a 2.4 lb average.

Top honors went to Pete McGinty and Shannon Larson with 27.625 lbs.  Second place went to Eric Spray and Adam Spray with 22.9375 lbs.  Third place was Corey Heinrich and Jerry Heinrich with 21.0 lbs.  Congratulations too the top three place winners and to all the other teams who fished this year.
