2021 Lac Qui Parle Walleye League

​

Week #1 Results

Anglers were greeted with cloudy skies, wind and rain.  30 fish were caught for a total of 58.375 lbs.  First place went to Jon Jerve and Tony Ohmacht who weighed 4 fish for 11.125 lbs and also had the big fish of the night with a 3.79 lber.  Second place went to Matt Fischer and Jeff Reike who weighed 3 fish for 7.5625 lbs.  Third place was Josh Weckwerth and Trent Weckwerth who weighed 3 fish for 6.375 lbs.  Fourth place went to Logan Spray and Mikkal Erickson who weighed 2 fish for 6.25 lbs.  Fifth place was Eric Spray and Adam Spray who weighed 3 fish for 6.0025lbs.  Rounding out the top 6 places was Tom Ludvigson and Dustin Fernholz who weighed 2 fish for 6.0006 lbs.

​

Week #2 Results

50 fish were weighed in for 122.5625 lbs.  First Place went to Josh and Trent Weckwerth who weighed 5 fish for 14.125 lbs.  Second Place went to Jon Jerve and Tony Ohmacht who weighed 4 fish for 13.5 lbs.  Third Place went to Eric Spray and Adam Spray who weighed 5 fish for 12.0625 lbs.  Fourth Place was Steve Dahle and Jamie Jahn weighing 4 fish for 10.0625lbs.  Fifth Place went to Trent Jorgenson and Matt Norby who weighed 4 fish for 9.1875 lbs.  Rounding out the top 6 places was Corey Heinrich and Jerry Heinrich who weighed 1 fish for 8.5625 lbs.  Big Fish pot went to Pete McGinty and Shannon Larson with a 4.375 lb walleye, as some teams did not put in for big fish of the night.

​ Week #3 Results

Week  three of fishing league saw 84 walleyes come to the scale for 181.1875 lbs.  First place went to Josh and Trent Weckwerth who weighed 5 fish for 15.1875 lbs.  Second Place was Cody Enger and Sean Hauck weighing 5 fish for 14.1875 lbs.  Third Place went to Tom Ludvigson and Dustin Fernholz who weighed 5 fish for 14.125 lbs.    Fourth Place went to Sam Thomas and Scott Van Overbeke who weighed 5 fish for 13.4375 lbs.  Fifth Place was Adam Moen and Dane Prestholdt weighing 2 fish for 12.375 lbs, including the big fish of the night an 11.125 lber.  Rounding out the top 6 places was Pete McGinty and Shannon Larson who weighed 5 fish for 12.25 lbs.

​Week #4 Results

Week  four of fishing league brought 24 walleyes to the scale for 55.0625 lbs.  First place went to Steve Dahle and Jamie Jahn who weighed 3 fish for 12.5625 lbs., including the big fish of the night an 6.3125 lber.  Second Place was Mikkal Erickson and Logan Spray weighing 2 fish for 9.4375 lbs.  Third Place went to Sam Thomas and Scott Van Overbeke who weighed 3 fish for 7.5 lbs.    Fourth Place went to Eric and Adam Spray who weighed 2 fish for 4.375 lbs.  Fifth Place was Tom Ludvigson and Dustin Fernholz weighing 2 fish for 4.125 lbs.  Rounding out the top 6 places was Matt Fischer and Jeff Reike who weighed 2 fish for 3.375 lbs.​​ ​

**Week #5 Results**

​​ ​

Week  five of fishing league saw 47  walleyes brought to the scale for 103.75 lbs.  First place went to Tom Ludvigson and Dustin Fernholz who weighed 5 fish for 15.125 lbs.Second Place was Mikkal Erickson and Logan Spray weighing 4 fish for 11.1875 lbs.  Third Place went to Corey Joyce and Sub (Chris Joyce) who weighed 2 fish for 9.5 lbs.    Fourth Place went to Pete McGinty and Shannon Larson who weighed 2 fish for 8.5 lbs, including the big fish of the night a 6.5 lber.  Fifth Place was Eric and Adam Spray weighing 4 fish for 7.1875 lbs.  Rounding out the top 6 places was Dean Broin and Jerod Zimbelman who weighed 2 fish for 6.5 lbs.​​ ​

Next week will be the conclusion of fishing league for 2021.

**​**

End of the Year Results

That concludes the 2021 Lac qui Parle Walleye League.  275 walleyes were weighed for 619.51 lbs, a 2.25 lb average.  Congratulations to this year's league champions, Eric Spray and Adam Spray with 52.7525lbs.  Second place was Josh Weckwerth and Trent Weckwerth with 48.5625 lbs.  Third Place went to Logan Spray and Mikkal Erickson 45.6875 lbs.
