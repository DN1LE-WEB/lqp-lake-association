About the Association

Welcome to The Lac Qui Parle Lake Association

Walleye and waterfowl are two things that come to mind when outdoorsman around Minnesota hear the words Lac Qui Parle Lake. Lac Qui Parle has a tradition of being one of the best walleye fisheries in the state.

The Lac Qui Parle Lake Association was created in 1982 as a non-profit organization working in conjunction with the Minnesota DNR.
