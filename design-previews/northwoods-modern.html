About

Walleye, Waterfowl & Community

Walleye and waterfowl are two things that come to mind when outdoorsmen around Minnesota hear the words Lac Qui Parle Lake. We've kept the tradition of world-class walleye fishing alive.

The Association works in conjunction with the Minnesota DNR providing funding for walleye stocking programs, fishing piers, boat ramps and shoreline protection.
