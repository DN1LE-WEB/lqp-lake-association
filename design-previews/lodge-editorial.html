About the Association

Walleye, Waterfowl & Community Since 1982

Since its inception, the Lac Qui Parle Lake Association has been involved in funding numerous lake projects. We work in conjunction with the Minnesota DNR to provide funding for walleye stocking programs, fishing piers, boat ramps and rip rap projects.

Our goal is to stock 25,000 walleyes and keep Lac Qui Parle one of the best fisheries in the state.
