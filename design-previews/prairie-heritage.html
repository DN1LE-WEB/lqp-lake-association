Walleye, Waterfowl & Community

Walleye and waterfowl are two things that come to mind when outdoorsmen around Minnesota hear the words Lac Qui Parle Lake. We've been keeping this tradition alive since 1982.

Working with the Minnesota DNR, we fund walleye stocking programs, fishing piers, boat ramps and shoreline protection projects.
